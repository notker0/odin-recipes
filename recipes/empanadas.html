Empanadas

Description

An empanada is a type of baked or fried turnover consisting of pastry and filling, common in Spanish, other Southern European, Latin American, and the Philippine cultures. It's typically made with cheese, chicken, or beef filling, but anything be stuffed in this tasty pastel.

This recipe will be covering how to make a fried empanada, but several other guides like this one exist all over the internet. Let's get started!
